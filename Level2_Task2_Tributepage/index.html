Members

Jin

The eldest of all seven, born on 4th December 1992. His full name is Kim Seokjin.
He is the visual and vocal of the group. You can enjoy his silver voice through his solos like Awake, Epiphany, Tonight, Abyss, Moon, Astronaut and many more.
He is funniest and also a very hardworking person.

Suga

The savage and second oldest of the group, born on 9th March 1993.
He goes with names Suga, AgustD, Yoongi, etc but his full name is Min Yoongi. He is the rapper and producer.
He has composed songs like Tomorrow, Autumn leaves, The Last, People, So far away,etc through which he comforts us and also songs like Daeng, AgustD, etc in which he is savage.

J-Hope

The Hope and sunshine of the group, born on 18th February 1994. His full name is Jung Hoseok.
He is the rapper and also the main dancer of the group.
He has always showed his bright side through his songs which are Daydream, Just Dance, Hey Mama, Ego and many more.
He is also known as the dancing machine of the group.

RM

He is the leader of the group born on 12th September 1994. His full name is Kim Namjoon and he likes it when people call him 'Joon'
He is the rapper and also has composed many songs.
You can listen to his poetic lyrics in songs like Just One Day, I like u, Converse High, Best of Me and many more.

Jimin

He is vocalist of the group, born on 13th October 1995.His full name is Park Jimin.
He is also called as the angel of the group and his eye smile is attractive.
You can hear his angelic voice in his solos like Serendipity, Promise, Christmas Love, etc.

V

He is vocalist of the group, born on 30th December 1995. His full name is Kim Taehyung.
He is known for his deep voice with which he can comfort you and also has been awarded as the most handsome person in the world for years now.
His songs Snow Flower, Winter, Sweet Night, etc will make you realise how his deep voice can calm you.

Jungkook

The golden maknae of the group, also the vocals, born on 1st September 1997. His full name is Jeon Junkook.
His heavenly voice will make u melt like butter. You can find confort and joy in his songs like Euphoria, My You, Still with you and more.
He is all rounder; he sings, raps, dances,composes and also good in drawing, sports and more artistic works.
